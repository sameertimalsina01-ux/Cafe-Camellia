Our story

A calm corner in the heart of Pokhara

Cafe Camellia is known for its spacious, comfortable seating, warm interiors, and a menu that keeps regulars coming back — especially for the cheesecake.

Whether you're after a quiet breakfast, a working coffee, or a place to catch up with friends, we've got indoor and outdoor seating to match the mood.
